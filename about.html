About Norivé

Learn about our mission, our team, and the story behind the seaweed-based edible bubble.

Our Story

Norivé was founded with a simple yet revolutionary idea: to create a sustainable, delicious, and innovative way to consume nutrients. Our seaweed-based edible bubbles are the result of years of research and development, combining cutting-edge food science with a deep respect for natural ingredients.

What started as a small experiment in a kitchen has grown into a mission to transform how people think about nutrition, sustainability, and the joy of eating.

Our Values

Sustainability

We believe in creating products that are good for people and the planet. Our seaweed-based ingredients are sustainably sourced and our packaging is designed to minimize environmental impact.

Innovation

We're constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in nutrition and food technology, creating new and exciting ways to nourish our bodies.
